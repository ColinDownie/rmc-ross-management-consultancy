Services

Clear structure. Confident leadership. Decisions that move the organisation forward.

Core Services

Ross Management Consultancy provides structural, operational, and leadership clarity for organisations that need to scale without losing control. Every service is designed to remove friction, reduce dependency on individuals, and create systems that work even when the founder isn’t in the room.

1. Organisational Structure Design

Designing the architecture of the organisation: roles, reporting lines, spans of control, and decision rights. This includes:

  • Leadership team structure
  • Departmental design
  • Role clarity and accountability mapping
  • Escalation pathways

The outcome: a structure that supports growth instead of resisting it.

2. Decision-Making Frameworks

Most organisations don’t have a decision problem — they have a clarity problem. RMC builds frameworks that define:

  • Who decides what
  • What requires consultation
  • What requires approval
  • What can be delegated

This removes bottlenecks and empowers leaders to act with confidence.

3. Leadership Operating Systems

Establishing the rhythms and routines that keep leadership aligned and effective. This includes:

  • Leadership meeting design
  • Reporting cadences
  • Quarterly and annual planning cycles
  • Performance and accountability systems

The result: leaders who operate as a team, not as individuals in parallel.

4. Governance & Board Alignment

For organisations with boards, investors, or advisory groups, RMC provides clarity on:

  • Board structure and responsibilities
  • Information flow and reporting
  • Decision boundaries between board and leadership
  • Governance frameworks that support—not hinder—execution

5. Organisational Diagnostics

A structured assessment of how the organisation currently functions, identifying:

  • Structural friction points
  • Leadership gaps
  • Decision bottlenecks
  • Operational inefficiencies
  • Misaligned incentives or unclear ownership

This forms the foundation for targeted, high‑impact interventions.

6. Founder Transition & Succession Support

Helping founders move from “doing everything” to leading strategically. This includes:

  • Delegation frameworks
  • Leadership team formation
  • Succession planning
  • Role redefinition for founders and CEOs

The goal: a business that no longer relies on one person to function.

Engagement Models

  • Diagnostic + Blueprint: A full organisational assessment and structural redesign.
  • Leadership Operating System Build: Designing and embedding leadership rhythms.
  • Ongoing Advisory: Monthly or quarterly support for leadership teams and boards.
  • Founder Transition Support: Helping founders step into strategic leadership.
